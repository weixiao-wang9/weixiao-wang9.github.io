# Gaussian Integral

Here is a beautiful equation for my first note.

The Gaussian integral is given by:

$$
\int_{-\infty}^{\infty} e^{-x^2} \, dx = \sqrt{\pi}
$$

## Why it matters?

It is fundamental in **probability theory** and **quantum mechanics**.
